Sony PS4

GameConsolePlayStation 4 (referred to as PS4), is launched by SONY SCE home game consoles. PlayStation game series is the fourth generation console.PlayStation 4 uses x86-64 processor based on AMD, and using Cell architecture PlayStation 3.

The purpose of replacing the processor architecture is to make the game development process easier to attract more diverse developers to join. This change is derived from SONY's experience in the development, manufacture and sale of PlayStation 3. PlayStation 4 other important hardware features include built-in 8 GB GDDR5 integrated memory, more rapid and devoted to Blu ray CD-ROM, audio, video, and other background service customization processing chip.

  • processor PlayStation 4 uses APU processor by SONY and AMD cooperation development, the integration of CPU, GPU and sound card.
  • Controller The main controller is PlayStation 4 DualShock 4, 2.1+EDR and the host computer via Bluetooth link, DualShock 4 adds a number of new features, including a positive pressure double point capacitive sensing touch pad.
